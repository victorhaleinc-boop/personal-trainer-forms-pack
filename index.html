FAQ

Common personal trainer intake questions

Can I copy this into Google Forms or Jotform?
Yes. The wording is plain enough to paste directly into a digital form builder, then adjust to match your service style and local compliance requirements.
Should I use this instead of a PAR-Q?
No. Intake and PAR-Q do different jobs. Intake collects background and goals; PAR-Q is the screening form that helps identify when medical clearance is needed. The paid pack includes both.
Do online trainers need this too?
Usually yes. Remote coaching still carries risk, and collecting history, goals, equipment access, and limitations makes programming far cleaner from day one.
What other forms do new trainers usually forget?
The most commonly skipped documents are the waiver, package agreement, and session notes log. Those are exactly the operational gaps the full forms pack closes.
